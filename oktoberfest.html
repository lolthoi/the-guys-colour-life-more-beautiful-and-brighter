Oktoberfest (German pronunciation: [ɔkˈtoːbɐˌfɛst]) is the world's largest Volksfest (beer festival and travelling funfair). Held annually in Munich, Bavaria, Germany, it is a 16- to 18-day folk festival running from mid or late September to the first Sunday in October.

What is Oktoberfest ?

Also known as the October festival, Oktoberfest is held annually to honor the brewing industry in the world. Not only is the gathering place for German beer lovers but also a meeting place for different culinary cultures

History of Oktoberfest

Prince Ludwig or King Ludwig I married Princess Therese on October 12, 1810. All citizens of Munich at that time were invited to attend the field in front of the city gate. city ​​to celebrate this great event. One of the birth festivals is Oktoberfest, also known as October festival. Through more than 150 times of opening, Oktoberfest has gradually become one of the indispensable cultural features for the German people in particular and the whole world. in general

Meaning of Oktoberfest

The Oktoberfest Munich Beer Festival is an opportunity for fans all over the country to gather together to enjoy excellent beer, join exciting games and celebrate the brewing industry around the world.
